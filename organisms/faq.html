How to Order

Follow the on-screen instructions to place your order. Once submitted, you'll receive an email confirmation with your Basket details.

Minimum Order Quantity

The minimum order quantity for each product is listed on its product page. For On Demand products, the minimum order is the first price break shown.

Custom Quantities

For On Demand products, you can order any quantity above the listed minimum. The quantities shown are just price break levels.

Benefit in Kind

Benefit in Kind refers to any non-cash benefits or gifts provided by an employer to their employee(s) that have a monetary value. Please note that any purchase from the Boehringer Collection for employees using a Boehringer cost center (PO purchase) will be considered as a Benefit in Kind.

You are expected to ensure that local tax regulations and implications are adhered to. In doubt, please check with your local tax expert.
