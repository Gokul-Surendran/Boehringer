Sustainability credentials explained

Each product has been tagged with sustainability credentials. Allowing you to make informed decisions when shopping.

MADE IN THE UK

This product has been made in the UK.

REUSABLE

This product can be reused again and again.

RECYCLED MATERIALS

This product has been made using recycled materials.

TREE PLANTING DONATION

Since 2021, we've partnered with Ecologi, an environmental organisation and fellow B Corp, that plants a tree for every order you place with us. Through this, you're helping us accelerate to a more sustainable future.

VEGAN

This product is vegan as it does not contain any animal extracts or animal by-products in the product or manufacturing process.

ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT

Economic empowerment is where a company empowers people with little resources through education, employment, health services, and giving them a sense of identity and community in which they can thrive and grow.

CARBON NEUTRAL

The product is carbon neutral as it balances the carbon dioxide it releases into the atmosphere through its everyday activities with the amount it absorbs or removes from the atmosphere.

CIRCULAR ECONOMY

A circular economy reduces material use, redesigns materials, products and ser-vices to be less resource intensive, and recaptures "waste" as a resource to manufacture new materials and products.

MONO MATERIALS

A mono-material product is a product which is only composed of a single type of material (Zips, Buckles etc are not included).

SUSTAINABLE FOREST

This product has been made with sustainably sourced wood which minimises the impact of logging on forests and the surrounding wildlife and communities.

PLASTIC FREE PACKAGING

No plastic has been used in the packaging (inner and outer).

SOCIAL ENTERPRISE

Social enterprises are businesses that put the interests of people and planet ahead of shareholder gain. These businesses are driven by a social/ environmental mission and reinvest profits into creating positive social change.

RECYCLABLE

This product can be easily recycled.

ENVIRONMENTAL CERTIFICATES

Environmental certifications monitors and helps improve environmental practice of the product, they are renewed annually to make sure the product is still meeting the standards.

MADE IN EUROPE

This product has been made in Europe.

ORGANIC

This product has been grown without harmful chemicals, leaving the soil, air and water free from contaminants that can cause harm.

SOCIAL COMPLIANCE

Social compliance refers to a set of ethical and moral standards that companies must follow in order to ensure that their products are produced in a manner that is responsible, sustainable, and safe for workers, consumers, and the environment.

ENVIRONMENTAL PACKAGING

Sustainable packaging creates less of a negative impact on the environment with regards to manufacturing, production, shipping and disposal or recycling.

PLASTIC FREE

This product has been made without plastic, including PLA.

DIVERSE BUSINESS / SUPPLIER DIVERSITY

Supplier diversity is where a business which is at least 51% owned and operated by an individual or group that is part of a traditionally underrepresented or underserved group.

TRIMAN

The French Decree No. 2014-1577 has made it mandatory to print the Triman Logo on recyclable products and packaging sold to consumers in France. Products and packaging with the Triman Logo on them are meant to be collected in separate waste streams. If the symbol is not there, the product can be disposed of in the general waste.

COMPOSTABLE

This product has been made with certain materials that have been certified to break down completely into non-toxic components (water, carbon dioxide and biomass) that will not harm the environment, given that the product is in the right conditions.

TRANSPORT - ROAD, SEA, RAIL

The product has been transported using either Road, Sea or Rail

CARBON FOOTPRINT

A product carbon footprint measures the total greenhouse gas emissions generated by this product, this can be from extraction of raw materials, to end- of-life.

DOUBLE DONATION

Double donation means the product has donated twice, the first one is having a tree planted for your order and the second is either giving back to charity, community or also planting another tree.

BIODEGRADABLE

This product has the ability to be fully broken down naturally by the organisms in an ecosystem.
