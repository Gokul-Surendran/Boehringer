At Boehringer Ingelheim we are driven by the desire to serve humankind by improving human and animal health. As a successful, family owned company we plan in generations. We work together globally and with integrity.

We take a considered approach towards our collection, ensuring our identity remains true globally whilst keeping sustainability at the heart of our decisions.

We work closely with our certified B Corp partner Fluid to develop and support this collection. Their approach to responsible ethical sourcing aligns with our own values at Boehringer Ingelheim.
