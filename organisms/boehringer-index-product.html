Our Ocean Bottle

The double wall insulation keeps your cold drinks cold and your hot drinks hot. Designed with a dual opening function the leak proof lid gives you the option to sip from the spout. Easy access for filling, cleaning and adding ice.

Bottles are made from 90% recycled stainless steel and recycled materials and are 100% dishwasher safe - all while helping protect the ocean, each Ocean Bottle funds the collection of 1000 ocean bound plastic bottles.
